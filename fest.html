Who We Are

Infinito Comics is one of the India's most prominent character-based entertainment company, built on a library of more than 2,500 upcoming characters and will be featured in a variety of media containing Comic Books, Animation, Games, Movies, TV Series and many more in upcoming months. Infinito utilities its character franchises in entertainment, licensing and publishing.

The Infinito Comics Universe is an Indian Media Franchisee and shared universe centred on a series of comic super hero's developed at Watermark Studio. The universes are based on characters that created and developed from a national competition 'Your Dream Super Heroes' published by Water Mark Studio. The franchise also includes television series, short films, digital series, and literature.

A set of games containing features of AR/VR will be published in upcoming days.
